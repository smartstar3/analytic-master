Starting your Feedback Analytics forms off with a welcome screen is a great start. Introduce people to your form and what they can expect.
To add a welcome screen to any form, open the Start Screen tab. A button to enable the Start Screen should appear. Click on this button.
Fill in the title and the text you want to display, for the users to read. The Start Screen automatically saves, so you can move out of this screen whenever you want. Don't forget to test your form before publishing it.
Click on the Disable Start Screen button if you decide to not have any start screen at all.
