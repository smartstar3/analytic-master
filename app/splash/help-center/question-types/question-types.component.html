Forms created with Feedback Analytics, exist out of questions. There are many different question types, here are all the question types listed below.
You'll find all the questions in the dashboard. To get there, log in to your account. If you dont have a free account yet Here is how to set one up.
Log in to your account, and you'll land on the dashboard. To display all the question types, hit the + button.
When you want to give freedom in the answer, Open Questions are ideal. You can ask the question and leave a small hint to guide people in the right direction.
When you want to say something, but don't need an answer, the Comments are what you need. Add a quote, a statement or another message for people to read. Comments can also be used as transition to the following questions.
For emails we have the Email Question type. These have build in email verification, so it only accepts correctly formatted email addresses. If people fill in a wrong format, they will see a warning text and can try again.
Also for Phone Numbers we have a question type with build in verification.
One of the most used question types is the Multiple choice. Use this if you want to offer a range of answers to a question.
Yes/no questions are questions are that can be answered with only a yes or a no.
Number questions only accept numbers as an answer. Great for asking ages, quantities or anything numerical. You can also select the ranges of answer possibilities. For example between -10 and 20.
For the Rating Question we have three different types. We have star, color and emoji rating. For each you can select the ranges yourself. So if you want a rating with 3, 5 or 10 choices, it's all possible.
The slider tool works just like the number question tool, it's easier to visualise the number you select, since you can see it proportionally to its range. You can select the minimum and maximum number of the slider question. Its also possible to select negative numbers.
