Welcome to Feedback Analytics! A great tool to collect data, handling feedback and much more! Information and customer appreciation are crucial aspects. We want to make this experience as easy and flexible as possible.
To create a form, you'll need a (free) Feedback Analytics account. Here is how to set one up.
Log in to your account, and you'll land on the dashboard. This is the place where all your Forms will be displayed. To create a new form hit the + button.
A pop up window will appear, here we can choose a name for the new form we are creating. Let's call it "Test Form" for now.
The newly created form is now visible in the dashboard. To start working on this form click on the Edit button.
We've arrived on the builder page. Here we can add different questions to the form, a start screen, multiple end screens and customize the design.
To add a new question click the + button on the the top right. This will slide out all the possible question options.

To add a question to the form, simply click on the type of question you want. Let's click on Open Question to start creating our sample form. When you're typing in your question on the right, you'll see a preview on the left.
To move to the next question, click on the + button. The question list will slide open again. Lets click on Multiple Choice, to add a new Multiple Choice question to the form.
You can add another Multiple Choice answer option by pressing enter after typing. For the tutorial I choose the title: "How did you find us?" and the choices Facebook, Instagram, Google and Word of mouth.
To use emoji, tap WIN + . on Windows or CTRL + CMD + SPACE on Mac to bring up a menu to choose from
if you want to work on an already created question, you can reopen the question, simply by clicking on the question again.
All questions are draggable. So changing the question order goes really easy.
Your Feedback Analytics form is now ready to be shared!
Click on the Test button if you want to test your form. This button is located on the right top side. When you're happy with the form and want to publish it click on the < button on the left top side of the screen to go back.
Go to Settings in the left menu to create a custom sharable link. Creating a custom link is only needed the first time you use our software.
Fill in your company name and change the share link to whatever you want. Note that share links are unique.
Click on Forms in the left menu, to go back to the dashboard
Click on the Publish Button to publish the form.
Click on the Share Button to retrieve the shareable link and on analyze to see the incoming answers.
You can now try your Feedback Analytics form with the retrieved link
